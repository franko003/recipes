Ingredients:
 - 3 lbs of chicken thighs
 - 1 lb of carrots
 - 4 stalks of celery
 - coconut oil

Directions:
 1. Put coconut oil into a large pan and set to low heat on the stove.
 
 2. Cut celery and carrots to desired size and add to the pan.
 
 3. Wash chicken and add to the pan and cover.

 4. Cook for 10-15 mins then stir everything around.  Cook another 5-10 mins and good to go.
